Pesto Pasta

Taken from All Recipes

Though sometimes not as appealing as other pasta dishes in terms of looks, pesto pasta is unrivaled in rich flavour.

Pesto pasta is luckily a straightforward meal...I mean it's pasta....with pesto.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons pesto
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (16 oz) package pasta
  • A pinch of salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until done. Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a frying pan over medium low heat. Add pesto, onion, and salt and pepper. Cook about five minutes, or until onions are soft.
  3. In a large bowl, mix pesto mixture into pasta. Stir in grated cheese. Serve.
